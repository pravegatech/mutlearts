About Us

'Like a plant needs supplement for its growth, individuals also need nourishment for improving their talents.'

Multeworld is a place where young talents are nourished and nurtured to get improved. We are committed to providing every possible assistance to the students to unfold their talent and upskill them. We believe that each child/student is unique with their own talents and skills, and is equally appreciable. Hence, our aim is to create a platform for various kinds of talents and thereby increasing our spectrum to cover more numerous talents and skills. Our talent platform provides different training programs for both technical and non-technical skills.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will I get a certificate after completion of every course ?

    Yes. If the course specifically mentions it or It is mentioned as a certification course in the title that you will get a certificate after completion of the course.

  • What are the timings for online learning?

    Timing is in the course details varying from everyone. You are advised to look at them carefully before selecting the course.

  • Can I pay in installments?

    No. We do not allow for the amount to be paid in installments.

  • How can I pay for the courses?

    We accept all payment options from credit card, debit card, UPI digital wallets.

  • Is every course is free?

    No course in Multeworld free unless mentioned as part of a free trial. Our tutors and the platform spend a considerable amount of money, time, and equipment to make high quality information available to you and that comes with reasonable cost for the effort and knowledge given.

  • How to take a Course?

    Go to Category, then choose your favorite skills and select the book course option, make the payment for your course to begin.
