About Founder

Veena Puppala is a visionary Entrepreneur, a professional Mind Coach, a Motivational Speaker, and a passionate Graphologist. She is the CEO and founder of one of the leading talent management organizations known as Multeworld. She has reached around 10K of students and has trained them about personal and professional aspects of life. She has been practicing Graphology, the science of studying human behavior and character through handwriting analysis, for more than 7 years now. Apart from inspiring and impacting others' lives with the help of graphology, she has also found it as one of the potential professions which possess lots of career opportunities. Hence, she is on a mission to help the students, who are looking forward to pursuing graphology as their primary career path.
